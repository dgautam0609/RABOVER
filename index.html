Our Story

About R A B Overseas

Backed by over 20 years of experience in the rice industry, Rab Overseas was established with a vision to bring premium quality Indian rice to international markets with reliability, consistency, and professionalism.

We specialize in sourcing high-quality Basmati and Non-Basmati rice through a strong network of 30+ trusted rice mills across India, enabling us to meet diverse customer requirements while maintaining consistent standards in every shipment. Our focus is on building long-term global relationships through quality products, transparent business practices, and dependable service.

At Rab Overseas, quality remains our highest priority. Every order goes through a strict two-step quality inspection process — the first check is conducted during the sample selection stage, and the second verification takes place once the rice is delivered to us before packaging and dispatch. This ensures there is no compromise in purity, consistency, or quality mixing. From sourcing and packaging to final shipment, we work closely with experienced packaging partners and logistics networks to ensure professionally packed products and smooth export operations for clients.
